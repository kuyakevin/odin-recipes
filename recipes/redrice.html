Chamorro Red Rice

If you want to spruce up your regular serving of rice, then this easy recipe will be your go to for any big gathering!
An instantpot is preferred for this recipe

Ingredients

  • One pack of Mama Sita Achuete Powder
  • 2 packs of Goya Ham Seasoning
  • 1 pack of Goya Corriander and Annatto (Con Culantro y Achiote)
  • 5 pieces of bacon cooked and crispy
  • 5 tbsp bacon grease
  • 1/2 Large Onion, roughly diced
  • 1/2 tbsp Accent seasoning
  • 5 cups of White Rice (jasmine preferred)
  • 5 cups of water

Make It

  1. Mix all ingredients into the instant pot
  2. Push the rice option on the instant pot
  3. Put the pot into the instant pot cooker, close the lid, and come back when the rice is finished
  4. Make sure to fluff the rice once it is done cooking. Let it rest uncovered for 3 minutes before serving
